Leonardo da Vinci

An Italian polymath of the High Renaissance who is widely considered one of the most diversely talented individuals ever to have lived.

Leonardo da Vinci became known for his notebooks, in which he made drawings and notes on science and invention; these involve a variety of subjects including anatomy, astronomy, botany, cartography, painting, and palaeontology.

Here's a time line of da Vinci life:

  • 1452 - Born in Anchiano, Italy
  • 1519 - Died in Clos-Lucé, France
  • mid-1460s - Leonardo's family moved to Florence
  • 1478 - Leonardo received an independent commission to paint an altarpiece for the Chapel of St. Bernard in the Palazzo Vecchio.
  • 1480 -Leonardo was living with the Medici and often worked in the garden of the Piazza San Marco, Florence, where a Neoplatonic academy of artists, poets and philosophers organized by the Medici met.
  • 1481 - He received a commission from the monks of San Donato in Scopeto for The Adoration of the Magi.
  • 1482 - Leonardo was sent as an ambassador by Lorenzo de' Medici to Ludovico il Moro, who ruled Milan between 1479 and 1499.
  • 1490s -Leonardo had already been described as a "Divine" painter.
  • 1475 - Leonardo first gained attention for his work on the Baptism of Christ, painted in conjunction with Verrocchio.
  • 1480s - received two very important commissions and commenced another work that was of ground-breaking importance in terms of composition.
  • 1480-1490 -Saint Jerome in the Wilderness (Unfinished painting of da Vinci)
  • 1490 - The proportions of the human body according to Vitruvius') is a drawing made by the Italian polymath Leonardo da Vinci in about 1490.
  • 1483-1493 - is the name of two paintings by the Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci, of the same subject, with a composition which is identical except for several significant details
  • 1489-1491 -Leonardo's most remarkable portrait of this period is the Lady with an Ermine.
  • 1490s - Leonardo's most famous painting of the 1490s is The Last Supper.
  • 1505 - Leonardo was commissioned to paint The Battle of Anghiari in the Salone dei Cinquecento.
  • 1503-1516 - created by Leonardo in the 16th century is the small portrait known as the Mona Lisa or La Gioconda.
  • 1503 - Virgin and Child with St. Anne (is an unfinished oil painting by da Vinci)
